[[SECTION:arch_textbook_foster_1985:ch01:s01|1.1 Memory Bandwidth In Cache-Sensitive Pipelines]]
cache_sensitive ingest workloads hit memory_bandwidth when every stage rereads the same cache line and the accumulator cannot hide the fetch delay.

[[SECTION:arch_textbook_foster_1985:ch01:s02|1.2 Queueing Pressure In High-Fanout Dispatch]]
high_fanout dispatch turns into queueing_pressure when the front stage admits work faster than the shared decode queue can drain.
